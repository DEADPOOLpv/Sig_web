About us
The name "SigraSamyakII embodies the shared legacy of our founders, Major General (Retd.) Sanjeev Dogra and Dr. Saumya Singh, and Professor Anand Prakash reflecting principles of structure, precision, and ethical conduct. Derived from Sanskrit, "Samyak" signifies "order" and "correctness," underscoring our unwavering dedication to excellence
